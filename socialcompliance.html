PRECAUTIONS TAKEN BY SSD(ABL) TO AVOID CORONAVIRUS (COVID-19)

SCREENING AT THE MAIN ENTRANCE

All our staff members are being screened at the main entrance in three steps:
1.Checking body temperature of all entrants through health official’s recommended tools.
2.Once cleared, asking them to sanitize their hands with approved hand sanitizers. and finally
3.Giving them masks to wear before entering the office premises.

SANITIZER's AVAILABILITY ON EACH FLOOR

SSD(ABL) management has ensured placement of Hand Sanitizer dispensers on each floor allowing employees to make use of it while going to their workstations each time they enter the floor.



DISINFECTING THE PREMISES/FUMIGATION

To ensure premises stay disinfected, at all times, SSD(ABL) management has taken the measures of fumigating the entire premises with spray guns and other tools reducing the possibility of contracting Covid-19 to almost zero.

AWARENESS SESSIONS

SSD(ABL) management has gone an extra-mile in spreading the word by educating each staff member of Covid-19 spread control mechanics. Special training session for both the workers and the management staff has been carried out to avert any possibility of contracting Covid-19 due to lack of knowledge. SOP’s have been defined and communicated to ensure 100% compliance.

CORPORATE SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITY
(Distributing Ration to the Underprivileged)

SSD(ABL) has always been in the outlook of helping the needy at the time of distress and anguish. Taking Corporate Social Responsibility to the next level, SSD(ABL) has been generously distributing Ration Packs to the needy during these unfortunate times. This is an ongoing process whereby people are identified based on their lively hood and income and provided with necessities to combat this pandemic.
